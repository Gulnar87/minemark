Everyone needs education - even MINE MARK volunteers

This video shows how MINE MARK volunteers participate in a Mission Orientation Course provided by the Dutch Combat Engineers. These courses normally prepare soldiers for their dangerous missions; however, this time they provided the MINE MARK team with more knowledge about the classification of landmines and other explosive remnants of war.
